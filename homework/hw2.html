I chose this image for its compelling subject! Of all the photos I shot of Nimue, I felt this best represented diagonals, with her head perfectly framed by the lower curves of her body. I feel this image invites the viewer closer, to inspect the fine details of her fur. I used my tonal range changes to enhance the light and shadow to enhance these details. The image is scaled 5 inches high by 7 wide.

I chose this image initially with the intent of replicating the golden spiral, but in actuality I feel I came closer to the rule of thirds. The rose head occupies the northeast corner, the pinecone spans from north to west, and the circle of acorns helps complete the fluidity of the image. I spent some time in the arrangement of these items, and ultimately I am happy with the result, even if it is not what I set out to create. I used my tonal range changes to darken the image, enhancing the drama and allowing the shadows more presence. The image is scaled 1900x1080 pixels..
